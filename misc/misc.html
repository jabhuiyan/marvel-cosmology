Infinity Stones

Outside of Time and Space, there is a quarry of creation which separates all realities from the First Firmament. Under this Quarry, the true celestials create infinity gems for all the universes in the multiverse.

A brief explanation of all the infinity stones.

Each infinity stone holds mastery over a singular aspect of the cosmos. Together, rules fall away and nothing is impossible.

Each of the stones contains within them an infinite abstract realm.

The power stone contains representation of forces of power in the universe, including the Power Cosmic

The realm of the space stone is so vast and empty it puts Sleepwalker in a trance, who himself admits he is no stranger to traversing infinite planes like the mindscape.

The realm of time is acausal and started to literally break Sleepwalker’s being down, who himself is a being not bound by time.

Soulworld, the realm within soul stone, is so big it can contain Gamora’s warped universe which was made by folding Earth 616 universe in half on itself.
