About This Website

2023

Marvel Cosmology

This website is not meant to be an encyplodia for marvel, or carry all of its informations, at all. This is a passion project made and held together purely by the sleepless nights of one student. As such, it is nigh impossible for me to have a complete rendition of the evergrowing cosmology that is Marvel. However,

  • It is a rendition of all the major cosmological stuffs
  • It refrains from giving a verdict on the informations, so the readers can arrive to their own conclusions
  • It is your gateway to reading up on the cosmology by yourself

Needless to say, all images, scans and information rights go to Marvel and I do not own any of the assets present in the website.
