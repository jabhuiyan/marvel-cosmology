Dream Dimension

  1. Home
  2. Structure

The dream dimension can manifest unreality, and logic in the dimension transcends regular logic

Dreams shape reality/creation and even the word of god.

Just the heart of dreamtime, part of the dream realm, is so vast Amadeus Cho has problems comprehensing it, much less calculate a chart through it.

It also transcends reason and is an abstract realm.

All of creation in marvel came from a dream/idea of one being. As such, it contains The Book of Job.
