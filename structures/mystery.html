The Mystery

  1. Home
  2. Structure

The Mystery exists beyond the Far Shore and lower than The Abyss/the Mountaintop. The Mystery is also known as The White Hot Room.

It exists both inside the cosmos (the version that is within the Nexus of Realities) and outside of it (the true version).

White Hot Room contains Death's infinite tower, where each ROOM of the tower is an entire realm of metaphysical afterlife

The White Hot Room is a dimensionally transcendental place.

The White Hot Room powers the concordance engines.

It is "beyond the Beyond" and more powerful than the Beyonders.

The nexus of all realities appearing in various comics is only the shadow of the shadow of the shadow of the real thing. The nexus exists in no space and every place. Logic melts and dissolves at the passage of the nexus.

The nexus contains a place called the cosmic axis. It is the major meridian of power across the multiverse.

the cosmic axis is what gave elder gods such as Demiurge and Atum their powers.[Thor(1966) Annual 10]
